Sunny Santa Barbara

Swim in the ocean, ride your beach cruiser down State Street, & eat fish & chips at Shoreline Cafe!

If you're coming down from Portland, be sure to hang out in the sun, grab some brews and watch sunsets from above in mountains. Inspiration Point is a great place to watch daylight to nightfall, plus also getting a small hike in.

Places around town to visit:

  • Butterfly Beach
  • Inspiration Point
  • State Street
  • Paxti's
  • Leadbetter Beach

Where to Stay:

  • Spanish Garden Inn
  • Los Padres Campground
  • Four Seasons
  • Hilton

Summer is a great time to vacation down in Santa Barbara, but it's sunny year-round, so anytime really is the best time.
